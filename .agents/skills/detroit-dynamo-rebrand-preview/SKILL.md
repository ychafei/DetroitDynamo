# Detroit Dynamo Rebrand Preview Skill

## When to use
Use this skill when implementing or modifying the Detroit Dynamo rebrand preview for LCTrainings.com.

## Brand Principles
- Premium professional soccer identity
- Dark navy / black foundation
- Electric blue energy accent
- Silver / white logo and typography
- Clean, athletic, modern, scalable
- Avoid childish, generic, over-busy, or template-like design
- Do not overuse soccer balls, mascots, or skyline cliches

## UX Principles
- Preserve current LC Training website.
- Add preview experience non-destructively.
- Make it easy to compare current brand versus future brand.
- Prioritize mobile responsiveness.
- Keep CTAs connected to existing booking/contact flows.

## Components to Prefer
- Hero section
- Brand transition section
- Training pathway cards
- Kit/apparel preview
- Digital system preview
- Social/matchday preview
- CTA section
- Current vs future comparison

## Visual Treatment
- Use large uppercase headings
- Use wide letter spacing for labels
- Use subtle gradients and glows
- Use restrained motion/energy lines
- Use crisp cards and premium spacing
- Use electric blue sparingly for emphasis

## Do Not
- Do not replace the live brand globally.
- Do not delete current content.
- Do not break booking.
- Do not import random third-party logos.
- Do not use external image URLs unless already approved.
- Do not make the design look like generic esports branding.
